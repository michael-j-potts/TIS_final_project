( September 2017 ) ( Learn how and when to remove this template message ) Brian Read (born 7 March 1939) was an English cricketer . He was a right-handed batsman and right-arm medium-fast bowler who played for Cornwall . He was born in Camborne . Read, who made his Minor Counties Championship debut for the team in 1957, made his only List A appearance during the 1970 season, his last at the club, against Glamorgan . From the tailend, he scored a duck. Read bowled 12 overs in the match, taking figures of 1-30.
