Ditchmore Lane Ground information Location Stevenage , Hertfordshire Establishment 1921 (first recorded match) Team information Hertfordshire (1921–2000) As of 25 July 2010 Source: Ground profile Ditchmore Lane is a cricket ground in Stevenage , Hertfordshire .  The first recorded match on the ground was in 1921, when Hertfordshire played their first Minor Counties Championship match which was against Buckinghamshire .  From 1921 to 2000, the ground played host to 41 Minor Counties Championship matches and 2 MCCA Knockout Trophy matches. The ground hosted a single List-A match in the 1969 Gillette Cup between Hertfordshire and Devon . In local domestic cricket, Digswell Park is the home ground of Stevenage Cricket Club who play in the Home Counties Premier Cricket League Division Two.  The cricket club has existed since 1878 and is the oldest sports club in Stevenage.
